## About the Artist

Hi, I'm **Aaron (Ziyun) Liu** — a fine artist, painter, and draftsperson based in Tāmaki Makaurau (Auckland), New Zealand[cite: 1]. 

My artistic practice centers on **deep observational inquiry**. Whether exploring the psychological spaces of human experience or mapping the complex, structural geometries of the natural world, my work is a continuous study of form, light, and time.

### 🎨 Creative Practice & Mediums

My portfolio spans traditional oil painting, precise graphite studies, and contemporary digital illustration, moving between two core conceptual spaces[cite: 1]:

* **The Human Space & Narrative Realism:** Working with oils to capture raw human emotion, light (chiaroscuro), and atmospheric interiors. I am drawn to the quiet, vulnerable moments behind the scenes of creation and thought—exploring the mental landscape of the figure.
* **The Botanical & Structural Space:** Creating large-scale observational drawings of native New Zealand flora. This side of my practice focuses on linear complexity, texture, and the intricate negative spaces found in natural forms as they undergo transition and decay.
